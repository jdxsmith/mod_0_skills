Class Grill

Attributes:
    isClean (boolean)
    isOn (boolean)
    surfaceTemperature (integer)
    currentFood (string)

Methods:
    cleanGrill (changes isClean attribute to true)
    grillOff (changes isOn attribute to false)
    raiseSurfaceTemperature (raises surfaceTemperature by 5)
    addBurgers (sets currentFood to 'burgers')
